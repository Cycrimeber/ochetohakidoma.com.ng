Welcome to Ochetoha K'Idoma

About Us

Ochetoha K'Idoma Youth Wing is a Socio-Cultural Organization and the Umbrella body of all organizations of the Idoma ethnic nationality globally.

Our Mandate
  • Peace building.
  • Advocacy.
  • Agricultural advancement of the Idoma land.
  • Innovation.
  • Promotion of our Culture, tradition and traditional Outfits.
  • Job creation through technology.
Mission

As the Umbrella body across the globe, we will unify all of the Idoma Ethnic Nationality across the globe, preserve, protect and defend all that affect the Idoma Nation or any individual of the Idoma Ethnic Nationality.

Vision

Consult and innovate towards the socio-cultural, economic, technological, political and infrastructural advancement of the Idoma Nation.
